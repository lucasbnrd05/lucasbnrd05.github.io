Why a
landing page  ? 

The landing page is the perfect tool for presenting your project to your target audience, validating its relevance and creating a strong brand identity.

Opening a business is often a difficult time financially. You need to create your business plan, persuade your banker and attract your first customers. Every euro is important for many young projects. So the point of a landing page is to get low-cost visibility, take the time to check the economic viability of the project and generate money. After a few months, you'll have enough time to create a more sophisticated website.
