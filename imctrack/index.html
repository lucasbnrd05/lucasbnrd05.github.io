About IMCTrack

IMCTrack is an innovative mobile application designed to help users monitor their Body Mass Index (BMI), track their fitness journey, and maintain a healthier lifestyle. Through features like BMI calculation, progress tracking, goal setting, and food analysis, IMCTrack provides users with valuable insights to improve their well-being. Whether you're aiming to lose weight, maintain your fitness, or simply monitor your health metrics, IMCTrack is the ideal companion for your journey.

Environmental Impact

IMCTrack promotes sustainability and environmental awareness through features that encourage healthier living. By analyzing food composition, the app helps users make informed dietary choices that can reduce food waste and promote sustainable consumption. Additionally, our location-based sport-finding feature encourages outdoor activities, reducing indoor sedentary behaviors and contributing to better air quality. By fostering an eco-conscious lifestyle, IMCTrack supports biodiversity, healthier air, and improved overall well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does IMCTrack calculate BMI?
IMCTrack uses a standard formula that considers your weight and height to determine your Body Mass Index.

Is my data stored securely?
Yes, all your health data is securely stored and encrypted to ensure privacy.

Can I use IMCTrack on multiple devices?
Yes! With your account, you can log in from different devices and access your progress anytime.
