Automatic Detection of Driving Distractions

An intelligent embedded system to enhance road safety by detecting driver distraction.

View Project on GitHub

The Problem of Distracted Driving

Driver distraction is one of the main causes of road accidents. Whether it's the use of electronic devices, drowsiness, or other actions, lack of attention can have tragic consequences. Our system aims to identify these critical moments to alert the driver.

Key Features

Real-Time Monitoring

Continuous analysis of head movements, steering behavior, speed, and safe following distance.

Multi-Sensor Detection

Combined use of a gyroscope, steering wheel rotation sensor, speedometer, and distance sensor.

Graduated Alerts & Automated Actions

Warning lights, information display, and automatic brake activation in critical situations.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

Sensors (gyroscope, steering wheel, speed, distance) continuously collect data about the driver and the vehicle's environment.

2. Symptom Analysis

The system analyzes this data to detect symptoms of distraction such as abnormal head tilt, sharp turns, or insufficient safety distance.

3. Corrective Actions

Based on detected risks, the system activates alerts (lights, display) and can even engage emergency braking.

System Components

Sensors

Gyroscope

Detects head tilt (X, Y axes).

Steering Wheel Sensor

Measures steering wheel rotation.

Speedometer

Provides current vehicle speed.

Distance Sensor

Measures distance to the preceding vehicle.

Actuators

Warning Lights

Yellow and red lights for risk levels.

Display

For visible data and alerts.

Automatic Braking

Activates brakes at different intensities.
